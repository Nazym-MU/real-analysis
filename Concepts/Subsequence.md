Let $(a_n)$ be a sequence of real numbers, and let $n_1 < n_2 < n_3 < ...$ be an increasing sequence of natural numbers. Then the sequence $$(a_{n_1}, a_{n_2}, a_{n_3}, ...)$$ is called a subsequence of $(a_n)$ and is denoted by $(a_{n_k})$, where $k \in \mathbb N$ indexes the subsequence.
