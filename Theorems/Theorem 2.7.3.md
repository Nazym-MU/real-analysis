If the series $\sum^\infty_{k=1}a_k$ converges, then $(a_k) \to 0$.

**Proof:**
Consider the case $n = m + 1$ in the [[Theorem 2.7.2 - Cauchy Criterion for series]]. $\qquad \square$

Conversely, knowing $(a_k) \to 0$ doesn't imply that the series converges (see [[Example 2.4.5 - Harmonic series]]).
