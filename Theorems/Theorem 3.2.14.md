1. The union of a finite collection of closed sets is closed.
2. The intersection of an arbitrary collection of closed sets is closed.

**Proof:**
De Morgan’s Laws state that for any collection of sets ${E_λ : λ ∈ Λ}$ it is true that
$$\left( \bigcup_{\lambda \in \Lambda}E_\lambda \right)^c = \bigcap_{\lambda \in \Lambda} E_\lambda^c \qquad \qquad \left( \bigcap_{\lambda \in \Lambda}E_\lambda \right)^c = \bigcup_{\lambda \in \Lambda} E_\lambda^c$$
The result follows directly from these statements and [[Theorem 3.2.3]].
